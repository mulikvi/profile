While interning in 2018, coming across the term 'Business Intelligence’ made me realize that data is the new language of business. I believe organizations with more data-literate people are the ones that are going to succeed. Recently, listening to a TEDx talk titled ‘Relationship: Analytics and Business’ got me interested in how the terms Business, Analytics, and Data are related. I am aware of the increasing usage of data and analytics which will drive the future and an increasing amount of data requires smart techniques for storage. There are many real-life problems that can be solved using techniques in the areas of Machine Learning, Analytics, and Data. I wish to deepen my knowledge in Data Analytics, Engineering, Management and Machine Learning. Along with that, I want to use my previous experience in Business Intelligence for solving business and real-life problems.
